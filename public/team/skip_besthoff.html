Skip is a seasoned corporate development, strategy and venture investment executive. Throughout his career, Skip has been focused on developing growth platform software and Internet businesses.

He is currently part of the corporate development team at Fleetmatics (NYSE: FLTX) where he focuses on mergers and acquisitions in North America and Europe. Before Fleetmatics, Skip was CEO of InboundWriter, a venture-backed software company pioneering predictive analytics in content marketing.

Previously, Skip was a venture and private equity investor for 12 years with both Castile and Rho Ventures. At Castile, Skip focused on early-stage on software and Internet companies. His investments included ByAllAccounts (acquired by Morningstar) and Health Guru (acquired by Propel Media). He also supported Castile's investment in Optiant (acquired by Logility).

Outside of work, Skip is an outdoors enthusiast. He is also passionate about education and a trustee of KIPP Massachusetts, a leading charter school network.
